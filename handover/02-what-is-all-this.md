# What the pieces are

Four things keep the site running. Day to day, you only touch the last one.

- **GitHub** stores the site's files, and every past version of them.
- **Vercel** turns those files into the live website and hosts it, for free.
- **Your domain (Namecheap)** is your web address. It's the one thing with a yearly cost.
- **The editor (Keystatic)** is the screen where you change text and images.

How they connect: you edit in the editor, it saves to GitHub, Vercel rebuilds, the site updates. One GitHub login gets you into all of it, and everything is in your name.
