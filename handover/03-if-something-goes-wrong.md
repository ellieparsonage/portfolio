# Troubleshooting

Every change is versioned, so anything can be undone.

**A change hasn't appeared.** Give it a minute or two and refresh (`Cmd + Shift + R`, or `Ctrl + Shift + R` on Windows). If it's still missing, open **Vercel > Deployments** and check the latest build didn't fail.

**You want to undo something.** The clock icon resets unsaved edits. Older saved versions are kept in GitHub, or a technical person of your choice can roll it back.

**You can't log in.** Reset your GitHub password from its login screen. If it asks for a code, that's 2FA: use your authenticator app or a saved backup code.

**An image won't upload.** Use a JPG or PNG under about 10MB, and try again after refreshing.

**The site is down.** Usually a failed Vercel build, or a domain that's lapsed on renewal (see Accounts & renewals).

**Still stuck.** Message a technical person of your choice with what you were doing and a screenshot.
