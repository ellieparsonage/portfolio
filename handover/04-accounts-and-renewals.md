## Costs

Running the site is free. GitHub and Vercel both stay within their free tiers, and with no card saved they can't bill you. The only cost is the domain, around £10-20 a year at Namecheap.

## Renewing the domain

Turn on auto-renew at Namecheap so it handles itself, or renew manually before it expires. A calendar reminder a couple of weeks ahead is worth setting.

## Security

Keep your logins in a password manager, turn on 2FA for GitHub, and save the backup codes it gives you. Rather than share a password, add people as collaborators.

## Ownership

GitHub, Vercel and the domain are all in your name. A technical person of your choice can be added as a collaborator to help when you ask, but you remain the owner.
